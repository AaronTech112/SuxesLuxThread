Privacy Policy

At Suxes Lux Thread, we prioritize your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy delineates our practices regarding the collection, utilization, and protection of your data while engaging with our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you place an order or create an account, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number.
  • Payment Information: Payments are processed securely through Flutterwave, and we do not retain your card details.
  • Usage Data: We collect data regarding your interactions with our website (e.g., pages visited, duration of visits) through analytical tools.

2. Usage of Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To reach you regarding purchases or updates

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or lease your personal information. Your data may be shared solely with trusted service providers (e.g., delivery services, payment processors) to facilitate the completion of your order.

4. Security

We use secure technologies (SSL encryption, reputable gateways such as Flutterwave) to protect your information.
